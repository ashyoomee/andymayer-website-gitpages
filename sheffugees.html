Sheffugees

In 2016 I started organising a series of hackdays to develop a digital product to help local refugee organisations. Now in use by 20 organisations across the city, 3,000 people are benefitting including 900 destitute asylum seekers who have been refused asylum. The content is now managed by a team of volunteers and employees at local organisations. You can see it in action at https://asylumjourney.org.uk/
